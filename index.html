The Concept

In todays Internet, with tons of webpages and apps, its all becoming a blur. I noticed this, while designing my own website, and its got me thinking- whats next? Ill tell you, scroll down...

What to Expect

01 Clean Design

Goodbye distractions and annoying alerts and ads, for real.

02 Natural Animations

Sounds crazy, but once you feel it, youll wonder why this wasnt there in the first place.

03 Right to customise

Have it your way- no, really! Well, you could say so, if you wanna change up the animations, font, colour palettes, and even design cues.

Design Rules

Minimal Materials

Keep it simple, dont go hard on materials. This UX Language focuses on accessibility while also maintaining elegance throughout.

Quiet Shapes

The only thing that must be sharp is info, not design.

Rounded and adaptive shapes make browsing a luxurious experience.

Smooth Colours

Colour palettes are the most important factors determining the user experience.
